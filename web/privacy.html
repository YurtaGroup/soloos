Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 8, 2026

Solo OS ("we", "our", "the app") is built by Timur Mone. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data.

1. Data We Collect

Solo OS collects the following types of data:

  • Account data: Email address and display name when you create an account.
  • App content: Projects, tasks, habits, finances, ideas, contacts, standup logs, and circles you create within the app.
  • Usage analytics: Anonymous events such as screen views, feature usage, and session data. You can disable analytics in Settings > Privacy.
  • Device info: Platform (iOS/Android), app version, and crash reports via Sentry.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and sync your Solo OS workspace across devices.
  • To power AI features (daily digest, idea validation, standup analysis) via Anthropic's Claude API.
  • To send you notifications you've opted into (daily digest).
  • To improve the app through anonymous usage analytics.
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat and Apple/Google.

3. AI Data Processing

When you use AI-powered features, your input is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for processing. Key points:

  • Anthropic does not use your data to train their AI models.
  • Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS).
  • You must consent to AI data processing before using AI features. You can revoke consent at any time in Settings > Privacy.
  • Anthropic's privacy policy: anthropic.com/privacy

4. Data Storage

  • Local storage: Your data is stored on-device using SharedPreferences and FlutterSecureStorage (encrypted).
  • Cloud sync: If you sign in, data syncs to our PostgreSQL database hosted on Neon, served via Render.
  • API keys: Your Anthropic API key (if provided) is stored in encrypted device storage and never sent to our servers.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Anthropic: AI feature inputs, with your consent.
  • RevenueCat: Subscription status management.
  • Sentry: Crash reports and error tracking.
  • Circle members: Data you explicitly share via Circles (finance, work modules).

6. Your Rights

  • Opt out of analytics: Settings > Privacy > Usage Analytics toggle.
  • Disable AI processing: Settings > Privacy > AI Data Processing toggle.
  • Delete your data: Settings > Danger Zone > Reset All Data.
  • Delete your account: Contact us at timur.mone@gmail.com and we will delete all server-side data within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Solo OS is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption, JWT authentication, and encrypted local storage. Sensitive data like API keys and auth tokens are stored using FlutterSecureStorage.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
